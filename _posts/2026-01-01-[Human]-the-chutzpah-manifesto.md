## The Core Conviction
Cambodia does not need more "followers" or "managers." It needs **Problem Solvers** who view every obstacle as a temporary inconvenience. It needs leaders who understand that being "small" is an advantage, not a disability.

### The 4 Pillars to Take Away:
<div class="pillar-summary">
    <div class="pillar-item">
        <h4 style="color: var(--accent);">01. Rosh Gadol</h4>
        <p>Own the whole mission, not just your task. Responsibility is taken, never given.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="pillar-item">
        <h4 style="color: var(--accent);">02. Asymmetric Thinking</h4>
        <p>Don't fight the giant at his own game. Use tech, speed, and agility to bypass the wall.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="pillar-item">
        <h4 style="color: var(--accent);">03. Constructive Balagan</h4>
        <p>Thrive in chaos. If the plan fails, pivot. If the system breaks, hack a new one.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="pillar-item">
        <h4 style="color: var(--accent);">04. Tikkun Olam</h4>
        <p>Build things that matter. Your business should be a repair kit for the world.</p>
